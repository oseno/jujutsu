Curious about JJK?

Jujutsu Kaisen is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Gege Akutami, serialized in Shueisha's Weekly Shōnen Jump since March 2018..

It is also the bane of my existence and all I can think about. If you're interested in nostalgia, fighting and characters that would warm your heart, this is the right choice.
